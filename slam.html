In-Ground Basketball Systems: SLAM

The Slam includes a heavy 4"x4" 11 ga. steel, direct bury post. Our one piece post design eliminates the potential for joint deterioration common with sectional posts.

Various basketall backboards and rims are offered with the Slam so you can choose the Slam package that fits your family.
