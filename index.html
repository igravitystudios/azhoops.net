Who Are We?

AZ Hoops is the leader in sales and professional installation of in-ground and portable basketball hoops. We also offer swimming pool hoops. AZ Hoops has a wide variety of basketball systems to fit any home and budget. We carry a variety of backboards. We proudly feature First Team premium basketball equipment, made in the USA, carrying the best warranty on the market.

In-Ground Basketball Systems

Direct-bury poles in concrete or the unique anchor system allow perfect pole leveling and easy pole removal if you move. The in-ground basketball systems have acrylic or glass backboards with breakaway flex rims. Larger hoops have the rare First Team warranty that allows dunking activity as well as players hanging on the rim. Adjustable height for basketball players of all ages. Unlike department store hoops, First Team basketball goals add appeal to your home.

Portable Basketball Systems

No more flimsy portable basketball hoops that get blown over by the wind and damaged. With no hole to dig and no concrete to pour, you still can have a high quality basketball hoop within most homeowners' associations' regulations. First Team portable basketball goals, while rigid and sturdy, can be folded and rolled into your garage or storage area. First Team basketball systems are adjustable in height with choices of acrylic or glass backboards and breakaway flex rims.

Pool Hoops

No more sloppy, tin, round pole in a round, water-retaining rusty sleeve. First Team's Hydroshot swimming pool hoop is constructed of stainless steel and will never rust. With stainless steel anchor bolts, this hoop is as tough as it gets for rough swimming pool basketball. The Hydroshot pool hoop has the most over hang available for safe, unobstructed play from the cool deck edge. You don't have to like basketball to enjoy swimming pool basketball. Because the Hydroshot swimming pool basketball goal is height adjustable, it will provide hours of great fun for the entire family.
