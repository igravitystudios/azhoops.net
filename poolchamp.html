In-Ground Basketball Systems: POOL CHAMP

Most swimming pools are constructed with a basketball sleeve in the deck surface. The round sleeves are very accommodating to almost any hoop system with a round post. One of the problems with the sleeve is that they retain water. The steel posts off all hoops will rust and deteriorate over time eventually causing post to fail and sometimes allow system to come crashing down.

With the Pool Champ we utilize a aluminum post that will not rust. The Pool Champ is adjustable and has an adjustment range of 30 inches. Complete with an aluminum framed acrylic backboard and pro like flex rim the Pool Champ offers American made quality and great pool side appeal at an affordable price.
